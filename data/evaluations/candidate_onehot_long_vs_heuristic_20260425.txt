Conclusion:
  Candidate one-hot long failed promotion.
  Although training PolicyAcc improved from 15.94% to 42.90%, playing strength got worse compared with the 50-epoch one-hot candidate.
  This indicates that lower training loss / higher training-set top-1 imitation does not necessarily improve actual play strength with the current teacher data and MLP architecture.

Decision:
  Do not promote candidate_onehot_long to official weights.bin.
  Keep official data/models/weights.bin unchanged.

Next recommendation:
  Stop simply extending MLP one-hot training.
  Investigate validation split / overfitting and checkpoint selection by arena evaluation, or train/evaluate the existing CNN architecture in Python to test whether MLP capacity or inductive bias is the limiting factor.
