AlphaZero Policy Sharpening Overfit Experiment
Date: 2026-04-25

Goal:
  Compare overfitting performance on 2000 samples with different training target policy temperatures.

Baseline command:
  python train/train.py --data_dir data/selfplay --arch mlp --epochs 200 --batch_size 256 --lr 0.001 --device cuda --model_dir data/models --run_name overfit_2k_mlp --max_samples 2000 --scheduler_step 1000 --eval_interval 10

Temperature 0.25 command:
  python train/train.py --data_dir data/selfplay --arch mlp --epochs 200 --batch_size 256 --lr 0.001 --device cuda --model_dir data/models --run_name overfit_2k_mlp_temp025 --max_samples 2000 --scheduler_step 1000 --eval_interval 10 --policy_temperature 0.25

One-hot command:
  python train/train.py --data_dir data/selfplay --arch mlp --epochs 200 --batch_size 256 --lr 0.001 --device cuda --model_dir data/models --run_name overfit_2k_mlp_onehot --max_samples 2000 --scheduler_step 1000 --eval_interval 20 --policy_temperature 0

Results:
  policy_temperature=1.0:
    Final PolicyAcc: 0.2280
    Final Policy loss: 3.2627
    Final ValueMAE: 0.0573
    Final ValueSignAcc: 0.9810

  policy_temperature=0.25:
    Final PolicyAcc: 0.4830
    Final Policy loss: 2.6482
    Final ValueMAE: 0.0737
    Final ValueSignAcc: 0.9775

  policy_temperature=0.0 (one-hot best action):
    Final PolicyAcc: 0.8285
    Final Policy loss: 0.8511
    Final ValueMAE: 0.0765
    Final ValueSignAcc: 0.9745

Conclusion:
  MLP capacity is sufficient to memorize 2000 samples when the policy target is sharp.
  The original soft MCTS visit distributions are too flat for top-1 imitation to become strong: policy_temperature=1.0 only reaches 22.8% top-1 accuracy.
  Sharpening helps substantially, and one-hot best-action imitation passes the 60% sanity-check threshold by a large margin.

Interpretation:
  Candidate failure is likely caused by weak/flat policy supervision rather than a basic model or CUDA training failure.
  For the next full candidate, train with a sharper policy target first. The most direct experiment is policy_temperature=0.0 or 0.25 on the full 17147-sample teacher dataset, then evaluate with evaluate_ai before promotion.
