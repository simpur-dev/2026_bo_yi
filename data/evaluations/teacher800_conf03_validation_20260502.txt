Key finding:
  Teacher800 generated more high-confidence samples, but validation top-1 policy accuracy did not improve.
  The model still overfits: TrainPolicyAcc reaches 0.8081 by epoch 120, while ValPolicyAcc stays around 0.10-0.11.
  Best validation accuracy occurs very early, around epoch 20.

Decision:
  Do not export candidate_teacher800_conf03 for arena evaluation.
  Do not promote any weights from this run.
  Keep official data/models/weights.bin unchanged.

Interpretation:
  More 800-sim heuristic teacher data alone is not sufficient.
  The bottleneck likely involves target formulation, data diversity, validation split methodology, or teacher policy noise/ambiguity rather than only high-confidence sample count.

Recommended next steps:
  1. Implement game-level train/validation split instead of random sample split, to measure generalization across games more reliably.
  2. Add sample weighting by policy confidence/entropy instead of hard filtering.
  3. Try training on combined old + teacher800 data only after adding weighting and better validation.
  4. If evaluating a checkpoint anyway, use epoch 20 as the least-overfit diagnostic candidate, not the final model.
